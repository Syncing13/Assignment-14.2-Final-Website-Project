About

Hi, I’m Mitchel.

I’m an IT professional specializing in network security, digital forensics, and technology leadership. I help individuals and organizations streamline technical operations, improve security, and keep systems reliable.

I’m most comfortable when I’m troubleshooting the “weird” issues—network errors, account access problems, and software that breaks at the worst possible time. I like taking technical concepts and turning them into clear, practical outcomes.

What I’m focused on

  • Cybersecurity foundations through hands-on tool use (Nmap, Wireshark, Autopsy, Burp Suite).
  • Digital forensics workflows and rapid response (forensic USB toolkit, file recovery workflows).
  • Building learning systems — like my internal CTF framework (UATf) to make concepts approachable.

Why I like this field

When people think about cybersecurity, they picture code and red alerts. That’s part of it, but what hooks me is the creativity: designing clever solutions, reverse‑engineering problems, and staying one step ahead. Security isn’t just technical—it’s strategic and human.
